Food in Ireland

These are some of my favourite Irish foods!


Traditional Irish Breakfast

A "Full Irish" breakfast consists of sausages, rashers (bacon), eggs, black pudding, white puddig and toast. Often there will be grilled tomato as well as mushroom and baked beans. And of course, no breakfast is complete without a lovely pot of tea.


Bangers and Mash

This classic of sauages, mashed potato and gravy is not unique to Ireland, but what makes it special is the Irish sausages. Most countries have their own way of making sausages, and they are one thing I miss from home if I'm away travelling.
